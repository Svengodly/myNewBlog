I built my first PC in 2015. It has an i5-6600K, 32GB of RAM, and a 1070 GTX Ti GPU. Over a decade later, it is still serving me well and the only aspect that has changed is the switch from Windows to Ubuntu. One of my coworkers told me that their laptop was sluggish, saying that it could take several minutes to boot. I offered to give it a lookover to determine if there was anything that could be done to breath new life into it. That new life would be granted by exchanging the laptop's HDD for an SSD.
